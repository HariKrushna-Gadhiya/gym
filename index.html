FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the gym hours?

We are open 24/7 for all members. Staffed hours are from 6 AM to 10 PM daily.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per calendar year for medical or travel reasons. A small admin fee may apply.

Are classes included in the basic plan?

The Basic plan only covers gym floor access. Upgrading to Titan Pro gives you unlimited access to all group classes.
