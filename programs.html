OUR PROGRAMS

Dive deep into our world-class training options designed to forge champions.

Strength & Power

This class focuses on fundamental compound lifts: the squat, deadlift, bench press, and overhead press. Designed for those looking to build raw strength, increase muscle mass, and improve their power output. Our elite coaches will correct your form and push you safely past your plateaus.

  • Olympic Weightlifting focuses
  • Progressive overload tracking
  • Small group format (max 8 people) for personalized attention

Class Schedule

Mon / Wed / Fri: 6:00 AM, 5:30 PM, 7:00 PM

Neon Spin

Step into the dark room. Neon Spin is a high-intensity cardio cycling class set to pulsating EDM and hip-hop beats under immersive, responsive neon lighting. This isn't just a workout; it's an experience that burns up to 800 calories per session while building incredible cardiovascular endurance.

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) on bikes
  • Immersive audio-visual environment
  • Heart rate zone targeting

Class Schedule

Tue / Thu: 6:00 AM, 6:00 PM
Sat: 9:00 AM

Recovery Yoga

True strength requires proper recovery. Our Recovery Yoga sessions incorporate deep stretching, breathwork (pranayama), and mobility drills designed specifically for athletes and heavy lifters. Prevent injuries, improve flexibility, and center your mind in our serene, mood-lit studio space.

  • Vinyasa and Yin Yoga blend
  • Meditative breathing techniques
  • Injury prevention and mobility focus
